"Cabo Verde" a town in Minas Gerais (BR), "Mindelo" a town near Porto (PT). **Always
co-occur the place with "Cabo Verde"** in `<title>`, H1, meta and first sentence; lean
on hreflang + geo. Seed one unaccented "Sao Vicente" in body copy (users drop the tilde).
