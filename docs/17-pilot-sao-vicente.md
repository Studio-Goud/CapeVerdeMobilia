## 0. Purpose and scope

This is the execution plan for the **Phase 1 controlled pilot** on **São Vicente (Mindelo)**, run entirely as a **concierge MVP**: verification, listing intake, onboarding, matchmaking, procedure guidance and support are performed **manually by the team**, over **WhatsApp** and in person, using the platform as an internal tool first (canon MVP: "Concierge (manual first)"). Maximum duration **6 months**.

**Why São Vicente (ASSUMPTION/med).** Mindelo is Cabo Verde's cultural capital, the market is concentrated and walkable (concierge feasible with a small team), and it is among the islands first explored by international buyers alongside Sal and Boa Vista. Sources: capeverdeproperty24.com, bespacegroup.com (canon fact 7).

**Why now, mobile-first (FACT/high).** 73.5% internet penetration (387k), 115% mobile connections (604k), 49.9% social (262k). The pilot is WhatsApp-first and mobile-first. Source: DataReportal "Digital 2025: Cabo Verde", Jan 2025 (canon fact 1).

Companion documents: `16-go-to-market.md` (strategy), `18-product-roadmap.md` (build), `20-kpi-framework.md` (measurement).

## 3. Go / No-Go criteria (defined up front)

### 3.1 Pilot entry (Go to start)

Start only if all four are true: MVP MUST-scope live in production; concierge team staffed; WhatsApp support operational; legal/tax counsel retained.

### 3.2 Mid-pilot checkpoint (end of Week 12) — continue / adjust

**Continue** if ≥50% of the Week-12 interim targets are met (interim ≈ half of §1): ~20 verified professionals, ~30 listings, procedure library started (≥8 articles), ≥1 partner conversation credible, first matches occurring.
**Adjust** (change target group / value-layer emphasis, not geography) if supply is ramping but demand is not, or the reverse.

### 3.3 Pilot exit (end of Week 24) — Go / No-Go to Phase 2

**GO to Phase 2** if **all** of the following hold:

1. **Four canon conditions** cleared or clearly on track (executive summary §10): legal/tax verification underway/complete; demonstrated willingness to pay; trust cold-start solved (verified inventory exists and is used); brand/IP clearance obtained.
2. **≥60% of §1 targets (G1–G7) met.**
3. **≥1 information partner** in place (G3).
4. **≥1 paying business retained** beyond its first billing cycle (retention, not just acquisition).

**NO-GO / pivot** if after 24 weeks <20% of paying-business (G7) and match (G5) targets are met **and** no credible path to improvement exists. Pivot changes target group or value layer; it does not change the island mid-pilot.

**PAUSE-MONETIZATION (not the pilot)** if engagement is strong but willingness-to-pay is absent — keep proving the loop, defer the paywall.

## 4. Week-by-week execution plan (24 weeks)

Weeks are grouped into four 6-week blocks. Workstreams: **PR** partner recruitment · **CC** content collection · **LA** listing acquisition · **BO** business onboarding · **UT** user testing · **CM** community marketing · **WA** WhatsApp distribution · **EV** events · **PRESS** press · **AMB** ambassadors · **FB** feedback · **IT** product iteration.

### Block A — Weeks 1–6: Seed supply and trust

| Week | Focus | Key activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **1** | Launch internal ops | Stand up concierge queue/CRM; finalize verification checklists (L1–L3); WhatsApp line live (**WA**); recruit first 3 ambassadors from the team's network (**AMB**). |
| **2** | First professionals | Onboard first 10 professionals (agents, architects) 1:1; team builds profiles; begin L1–L2 verification (**BO**, **AMB**). Start procedure-library research from gov.cv/INGT/Conservatória/Casa do Cidadão (**CC**). |
| **3** | First listings | Concierge intake of first 10 listings (photograph, write, translate pt/en) (**LA**). Draft first 5 procedure articles (**CC**). Approach municipality for informal info partner (**PR**). |
| **4** | Trades + suppliers | Onboard plumbers, electricians, materials suppliers (L1–L2) (**BO**). Publish first procedure articles (**CC**). Small usability test with 5 professionals (**UT**, **FB**). |
| **5** | Landowners | Concierge intake of land listings; L3 title-doc checks *(legal verification required)* (**LA**). First WhatsApp broadcast of new listings (**WA**). Ship fixes from Week-4 testing (**IT**). |
| **6** | Block-A review | Tally: ~15 professionals, ~20 listings, ~6 articles. First feedback synthesis (**FB**); go/adjust micro-check. |

### Block B — Weeks 7–12: Invite demand, prove matches

| Week | Focus | Key activities |
|------|-------|----------------|
| **7** | Demand outreach | Begin foreign-prospect outreach (diaspora groups, buyer forums) via WhatsApp + community (**CM**, **WA**). Legal professional onboarded to co-author procedure content (**PR**, **CC**). |
| **8** | First matches | Human matchmaking: connect first foreign prospects to verified supply (**LA**→lead→**BO**). Log matches in admin. Recruit 3 more ambassadors (**AMB**). |
| **9** | Community event #1 | Verified-professional meetup in Mindelo (**EV**); doubles as content + press hook (**PRESS**). Collect first verified reviews from completed interactions (**FB**). |

| **11** | Press push | Local press coverage of a neutral trust-first platform (**PRESS**). Second WhatsApp listing broadcast (**WA**). |
| **12** | Mid-pilot checkpoint | Formal §3.2 review: ~20 professionals, ~30 listings, ≥8 articles, first matches + reviews, ≥1 partner conversation. Continue / adjust decision. |

### Block C — Weeks 13–18: Monetization test

| Week | Focus | Key activities |
|------|-------|----------------|

## 5. Workstream detail

### 5.1 Partner recruitment (PR)
Target ≥1 informal information partner (municipality / public body). Start informal using public sources; SLA and PPP are later-phase upside, not preconditions (canon government ladder; risk #4). *Government confirmation required.*

### 5.2 Content collection (CC)
Build procedure library v1 from **public sources only** — gov.cv (unified portal, launched 24 Feb 2026), INGT, Conservatória do Registo Predial, Casa do Cidadão, EASE (canon facts 2, 3, 6). Every article is **sourced, non-advisory**, and distinguishes official text vs plain-language summary vs translation (canon Languages). pt/en human; kea/nl/fr machine.

### 5.3 Listing acquisition (LA)
Concierge intake: team photographs, writes, translates and publishes. Land listings require L3 title-doc checks *(legal verification required — canon fact 3)*.

### 5.4 Business onboarding (BO)
Personal 1:1 onboarding; team does the work; verified badge is the reward. Paywall only after demonstrated value (canon sales approach; `16-go-to-market.md` §10).

### 5.5 User testing & iteration (UT/IT)
Small, frequent usability tests (5 users) after each block; ship fixes within the following week. Mobile-first, WCAG 2.1 AA (canon tech).

### 5.6 Community, WhatsApp, events, press, ambassadors (CM/WA/EV/PRESS/AMB)
WhatsApp is the primary distribution and support channel. Relationship channels (community, ambassadors) are weighted over paid ads in the pilot. No fabricated endorsements or partners (canon).

### 5.7 Feedback (FB)
Structured after every block; feeds §1 KPI tracking and the iteration backlog. Willingness-to-pay signals captured at onboarding and at paywall.

## 7. Risks specific to the pilot

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Trust cold-start (canon risk #2) | Block A seeds verified supply before Block B invites demand |
| Willingness to pay absent (canon risk #5) | Free tier first; paywall only on proven value; PAUSE-MONETIZATION rule (§3.3) |
| Legal/tax facts unconfirmed (canon facts 3–5) | Counsel retainer from Week 0; all content *verification required* |
| Partner does not materialize (canon risk #4) | Use public sources; treat SLA/PPP as upside |
| Concierge overload | 4 FTE cap; if queue exceeds capacity, prioritize high-value land/foreign matches |
