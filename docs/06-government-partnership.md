## 1. Core message

**Kavíla does not replace government. It makes official information more
findable, more understandable and more accessible.**

Cabo Verde is investing heavily in digital government — the unified portal
**gov.cv launched 24 February 2026**, replacing multiple platforms, with the
legacy citizen portal **"Porton di nôs Ilha"**, authentication and e-signature
via **Chave Móvel Digital (CMDCV) / Autentika**, the e-gov backbone operated by
**NOSi**, and a stated target of **60% of vital public services online by 2026**
and **>80% by 2030** (Canon KEY FACT 2 — FACT/high-med; sources: techafricanews.com
25 Feb 2026, ecofinagency.com, govinsider.asia, portal-autentika.gov.cv, nosi.cv).

Kavíla is an **independent** trust-first layer (Canon §Brand) that sits *beside*
these official systems. It:

- links to and cites official sources (never overwrites or re-authors them);
- adds plain-language summaries and multilingual access (pt/kea/en/nl/fr —
  Canon §Languages), always preserving and linking the official source text;
- guides citizens, professionals and investors *to the competent authority*
  rather than acting as one;
- surfaces where people get stuck, so public bodies can improve their own
  information.

Kavíla never issues decisions, never charges for access to official
information, and never alters the ranking or wording of official content for
payment (Canon §Business model; `05-business-model.md` §5 guardrails).

## 3. Three collaboration models

These are the Canon's three government models, elaborated. Progression is
expected: **(1) → (2) → (3)** as trust and scope grow.

### Model 1 — Informal information partner *(free / subsidised)*

| Dimension | Terms |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Kavíla publishes plain-language summaries that **link to and cite** the body's official information; no formal endorsement. |
| **Governance** | Light. Named contact on each side; informal review of published summaries. |
| **Responsibilities** | Kavíla: accuracy of summaries, prominent source links, prompt correction. Body: point to canonical sources, flag errors. |
| **Data ownership** | Body owns official content; Kavíla owns its summaries/translations and links back. No personal data exchanged. |
| **Liability** | Kavíla carries a clear disclaimer ("informational, not official; verify with the competent authority"). Legal verification required. |
| **Approval process** | Optional courtesy review; no sign-off gate. |
| **Costs** | Free or subsidised (Canon). No fee to the body. |
| **Service levels** | Best-effort; correction target e.g. 5 working days (RECOMMENDATION). |
| **Audit** | Change log of published summaries available on request. |
| **Exit** | Either side ends at will; Kavíla removes contested summaries promptly. |

### Model 2 — Official publication partner *(SLA licence)*

| Dimension | Terms |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | The body authorises Kavíla to publish/mirror specified official information with an "official source" designation, under a service-level agreement. |
| **Governance** | Formal steering contact; periodic review meetings; defined content scope. |
| **Responsibilities** | Body: provides/approves canonical content and updates. Kavíla: faithful publication, versioning, agreed uptime, correction SLAs, multilingual rendering with source preserved. |
| **Data ownership** | Body retains full ownership and editorial authority over official text; Kavíla licenses to display. Aggregated usage analytics shared with the body. |
| **Liability** | Defined in licence; Kavíla liable for faithful reproduction, body for source correctness. Legal verification required. |
| **Approval process** | Content sign-off workflow (gov editor → gov approver roles — Canon §Roles) before publication of changes. |
| **Costs** | SLA licence, **RECOMMENDATION 150.000–250.000 CVE/mo (€1.360–2.267)** depending on scope; may be subsidised/grant-funded. Government confirmation required. |
| **Service levels** | E.g. 99.5% availability, content-update propagation ≤ 2 working days, critical-correction ≤ 24h (RECOMMENDATION). |
| **Audit** | Full version history, access logs, annual review; body may audit. |
| **Exit** | Notice period (e.g. 90 days); orderly takedown/hand-back of published official content. |

### Model 3 — Public-private digital partnership *(PPP)*

| Dimension | Terms |
|---|---|
| **Purpose** | Joint delivery of a digital service (e.g. integrated procedure guidance tied to official systems), potentially integrating with the NOSi/Autentika/CMDCV ecosystem (integration **to validate**). |
| **Governance** | Formal PPP governance: joint steering committee, defined decision rights, independence safeguards so Kavíla's trust-first neutrality is preserved (Canon §Brand). |
| **Responsibilities** | Split by agreement; roles, KPIs and escalation defined contractually. |
| **Data ownership** | Data-governance schedule: official data owned by the body; personal data processed under CNPD regime (Canon KEY FACT 8); analytics rights defined; no resale of personal data (`05-business-model.md` §5). |
| **Liability** | Allocated contractually with indemnities and insurance. Legal verification required. |
| **Approval process** | Procurement/partnership process per public-sector rules; formal approvals and possibly parliamentary/ministerial sign-off. Government confirmation required. |
| **Costs** | Custom (revenue-share, availability payments, or grant). Government confirmation required. |
| **Service levels** | Enterprise SLAs with penalties/credits; security, DR and audit obligations (aligns with Canon tech stack: backups, monitoring, IR, DR). |
| **Audit** | Independent audit rights; security and data-protection audits; transparency reporting. |
| **Exit** | Defined termination, step-in and data-hand-back provisions; continuity plan for citizens. |

## 4. Independence & trust safeguards (all models)

1. Kavíla is **independent digital infrastructure** (Canon §Brand); partnership
   never implies government control of the platform or vice-versa.
2. Official information is **never for sale** — no payment alters its ranking,
   wording or prominence (`05-business-model.md` §5).
3. Official source text is **always preserved and linked**; translations and
   plain-language summaries are clearly distinguished from official text
   (Canon §Languages).
4. **No paid verification or paid reviews** anywhere on the platform.
5. Every guidance flow **refers users to the competent authority** and carries a
   clear "informational, not official" disclaimer.
6. Shared analytics are **aggregated and anonymised only** (CNPD — legal
   verification required).

## 6. Recommended engagement path (RECOMMENDATION)

1. **Pilot (São Vicente):** begin as **Model 1** informal information partner
   with one or two municipal/island bodies — zero cost, prove accuracy and
   usefulness, gather anonymised information-need insights.
2. **Scale credibility:** convert successful relationships to **Model 2**
   official publication partnerships under an SLA licence (a Y2 revenue line in
   `15-financial-model.md`).
3. **Strategic depth:** pursue **Model 3 PPP** only where integration with the
   NOSi/Autentika/CMDCV ecosystem and joint service delivery are warranted,
   preserving independence throughout.

All commercial terms and integrations above are **Government confirmation
required** and, where legal, **Legal verification required**.
