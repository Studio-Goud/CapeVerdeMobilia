## 0. How to read this document

This is the phased go-to-market (GTM) plan for **Ilhavista**, the trust-first digital infrastructure for property, building and public information in Cabo Verde. It follows the canon's **four-phase ambition** — pilot on São Vicente → multi-island → national + institutional → international replication — and the **concierge-MVP** operating model, in which every trust-critical function (verification, listing intake, onboarding, matchmaking, procedure guidance, support) is performed **manually by a human team first**, then progressively productized.

All financial figures are in **CVE**, pegged to EUR at **110.265 CVE = 1 EUR** (**FACT/high — still verify**). Team sizes, budgets and KPI targets in this document are **ASSUMPTION** or **RECOMMENDATION** unless cited otherwise; they are planning anchors to be replaced with validated numbers during the pilot.

**Non-negotiable guardrail (canon):** paid visibility **never** buys verification, review scores, or manipulation of official information. Sponsored content is always visibly labelled "Patrocinado/Sponsored".

## 1. GTM thesis and sequencing logic

**RECOMMENDATION.** Ilhavista's core promise is **trust**, not listing volume. A trust marketplace with no verified inventory is not trusted (canon risk #2). Therefore the GTM does **not** open with paid advertising to a cold audience. It opens with **concierge-seeded supply** — a human team manually verifying a starter cohort of professionals and listings — and only then invites demand.

The sequencing rule across all four phases:

1. **Seed trusted supply manually** (concierge verification L1–L3).
2. **Publish neutral information + procedure guidance** from public sources.
3. **Invite bounded demand** (foreign buyers first — highest uncertainty, highest willingness to pay).
4. **Prove the loop** (information → trust → findability → lead → match) before adding transaction/job monetization.
5. **Only productize a manual step once its pattern is proven and its volume hurts.**

**Why São Vicente first (ASSUMPTION/med).** Mindelo is Cabo Verde's cultural capital, the market is concentrated and walkable (concierge feasible with a small team), and it is among the islands first explored by international buyers alongside Sal and Boa Vista. A single island bounds legal, verification and content scope. Sources: capeverdeproperty24.com, bespacegroup.com (canon fact 7).

**Why the audience is reachable (FACT/high).** Internet penetration **73.5%** (387k users), mobile connections **115%** of population (604k), social media **49.9%** (262k). The plan is mobile-first and WhatsApp-centred by design. Source: DataReportal "Digital 2025: Cabo Verde", Jan 2025 (canon fact 1).

## 2. Target groups (Phase 1, São Vicente)

The pilot engages both **supply** (professionals, listings, information) and **demand** (buyers/clients). Concierge onboarding is applied to every group. Sequence and rationale:

| # | Group | Side | Why they matter in the pilot | First offer | Concierge touch |
|---|-------|------|------------------------------|-------------|-----------------|
| 1 | **Local real-estate agents (makelaars)** | Supply | Fastest route to credible listings; core future subscription base | Free verified profile + listing intake done for them | Manual profile build, L1–L2 verification |
| 2 | **Architects** | Supply | High-trust professionals; anchor the "build" value layer | Free verified profile + portfolio | Manual portfolio intake, L2–L3 |
| 3 | **Contractors / builders (empreiteiros)** | Supply | Core of job/quote demand later; reputation asset today | Free verified profile + reviews | L2–L3, reference checks |
| 4 | **Plumbers** | Supply | Everyday trade demand; density builds directory value | Free verified trade profile | L1–L2 |
| 5 | **Electricians** | Supply | As above; safety-relevant, so verification matters | Free verified trade profile | L1–L2 |
| 6 | **Building-materials suppliers** | Supply | Directory + lead value; anchor the materials layer | Free directory listing | L2 business verification |
| 7 | **Landowners** | Supply | Land listings are scarce and high-value to foreign demand | Concierge listing intake, free basic listing | Manual intake, L3 title-doc check *(legal verification required)* |
| 8 | **Municipality (Câmara Municipal de São Vicente)** | Information | ≥1 informal information partner is a pilot goal | Informal information-partner role (free/subsidised) | Relationship-led, manual content |
| 9 | **Legal professionals (lawyers, notary-adjacent)** | Supply / trust | De-risk foreign purchase; anchor procedure guidance | Verified profile + procedure-library co-authoring | L3–L5 where institutional |
| 10 | **Foreign prospects (buyers/investors)** | Demand | Highest willingness to pay for trust and guidance | Multilingual gateway + concierge matchmaking | Manual matchmaking, WhatsApp support |

**RECOMMENDATION on ordering.** Recruit groups 1–7 (supply) and 8–9 (information/trust) in the first half of the pilot so that when group 10 (foreign demand) arrives, the marketplace is not empty. This directly mitigates the trust cold-start risk (canon risk #2).

## 4. Phase 1 — São Vicente (controlled pilot, concierge MVP)

**Operating principle:** everything is manual. Verification, listing intake, onboarding, matchmaking, procedure guidance and support are performed by the team, over WhatsApp and in person, using the platform as an internal tool first (canon MVP: "Concierge (manual first)").

### 4.1 Objectives

- Recruit a **first cohort of verified professionals** across the 9 target groups.
- Acquire the **first property and land listings** via concierge intake.
- Secure **≥1 informal information partner** (municipality or public body).
- Build a **working procedure library v1** from public sources (gov.cv, INGT, Conservatória, Casa do Cidadão), clearly sourced and non-advisory.
- Produce **some successful matches** (foreign prospect → verified professional/listing → contact).
- Collect **some verified reviews** (L4-adjacent, transaction/interaction confirmed).
- Convert the **first paying businesses** (Pro/Business/Premium) to validate willingness to pay.

### 4.2 Activities

- Concierge verification (L1–L3, manual L3) of the starter cohort.
- Manual listing intake: team photographs, writes, translates and publishes listings for sellers/landowners.
- Personal onboarding: 1:1 sessions (in person or WhatsApp video) for every professional.
- WhatsApp support line as the primary channel (canon: WhatsApp-oriented notifications).
- Team-driven matchmaking: humans match foreign prospects to verified supply.
- Manual procedure guidance: the team walks users through buying/building/registering steps, non-advisory, sourced.
- Community marketing, events, press and ambassador recruitment (see §9).

### 4.3 Costs (ASSUMPTION — planning anchor, market validation required)

|-----------|---------------|----------------|------|
| Core team (see §4.4) | 900.000 | ~8.160 | Lean local team, blended |
| WhatsApp Business + comms tools | 15.000 | ~136 | Business API optional early |
| Hosting/infra (staging + prod) | 40.000 | ~363 | Modular monolith, small |
| Content/translation (pt/en human) | 60.000 | ~544 | Procedure library + listings |
| Events + community (per event) | 80.000 | ~725 | 1–2 events/month |

### 4.4 Team (RECOMMENDATION)

| Role | FTE | Responsibility |
|------|-----|----------------|
| Pilot lead / GTM operator | 1.0 | Owns targets, partnerships, decision gates |
| Verification & listing specialist | 1.0 | Concierge verification + listing intake |

| Local field associate | 0.5 | Photography, in-person onboarding, ambassador support |

Total ≈ **4.0 FTE**. Deliberately small — concierge is labour-bounded, and a small team is the point on a single walkable island.

### 4.6 Decision gate (end of Phase 1)

**Go to Phase 2** if the pilot clears the four canon conditions (executive summary §10): legal/tax verification underway or complete; demonstrated willingness to pay; trust cold-start solved (verified inventory exists and is used); brand/IP clearance obtained — **and** ≥60% of §4.5 targets met.

### 4.7 Stop / pivot criteria

- **Stop** if after 6 months <20% of paying-business and match targets are met **and** no path to improvement is evident.
- **Pivot** (change target group or value layer, not geography) if supply recruits but demand does not, or vice versa.
- **Pause monetization** (not the pilot) if willingness-to-pay signals are absent but engagement is strong.

### 4.8 Scale-up criteria

- Concierge steps show a **repeatable, documented pattern** ready to semi-automate.
- Unit economics of one verified professional and one match are understood.
- At least one information partnership is renewable.

## 5. Phase 2 — Multiple islands

**Objective:** prove the São Vicente pattern transfers to a second and third island, and convert the most painful manual steps into product.

**Geography (RECOMMENDATION):** add **Sal** and **Boa Vista** (international-buyer demand) and **Santiago/Praia** (largest domestic market and seat of national institutions). Source for island prioritization: canon fact 7.

### 5.1 Activities

- Replicate concierge playbook with a **local field associate per new island**.
- Ship the **first automations**: self-serve listing intake with concierge QA, self-serve professional profiles with concierge verification queue, procedure-wizard v1 to users directly.
- Launch **referral and ambassador programs** island-to-island.
- Begin **Casa do Cidadão / EASE** partner conversations for professional onboarding (canon fact 6).

### 5.2 Costs, team, KPIs

| Item | Detail (ASSUMPTION) |
|------|---------------------|
| Team | Grows to ~8–10 FTE: +1 field associate/island, +1 engineer, +1 verification specialist, +1 partnerships lead |
| Budget | ~2.5–3.5M CVE/month (~€23k–32k) blended across islands |
| KPI focus | Cost-to-verify per professional ↓; self-serve intake share ↑; cross-island match rate; paid-conversion rate ↑ |

### 5.3 Decision gate / stop / scale-up

- **Gate:** the second island reaches 50% of São Vicente's per-capita KPI ramp within 4 months.
- **Stop/pivot:** if concierge cost per verified professional does **not** fall as automation ships, halt expansion and fix the funnel.
- **Scale-up to Phase 3:** ≥2 islands self-sustaining on engagement; ≥1 official-publication-partner conversation credible; automated core stable.

## 6. Phase 3 — National + institutional

**Objective:** national coverage and a shift from informal information partner → **official publication partner (SLA licence)** → path toward **public-private digital partnership (PPP)** (canon business model, government ladder).

### 6.1 Activities

- Complete island coverage; concierge reserved for edge cases and high-value land/foreign deals.
- Pursue **official-publication SLA** with public bodies, leveraging the **gov.cv** digitization tailwind (unified portal launched 24 Feb 2026; 60% of vital services online targeted 2026, >80% by 2030 — canon fact 2, **Government confirmation required**).
- Integrate, where sanctioned, with **CMDCV** (Chave Móvel Digital) for identity/e-signature and **NOSi** e-gov backbone (canon fact 2, **Government confirmation required**).
- Introduce **facilitated-job take rate (5–10%)** once escrow exists (canon business model — deferred until this phase).

### 6.2 Costs, team, KPIs

| Item | Detail (ASSUMPTION) |
|------|---------------------|
| Team | ~15–25 FTE; dedicated partnerships, compliance, and finance functions |
| Budget | Scales with revenue; target contribution-positive on subscriptions before PPP spend |
| KPI focus | National verified-professional coverage; official-partner SLAs signed; take-rate GMV; CNPD/data-protection compliance closed (**legal verification required**, canon fact 8) |

### 6.3 Decision gate / stop / scale-up

- **Gate:** ≥1 official-publication SLA signed **or** a credible PPP mandate.
- **Stop/pivot:** if institutional partnerships stall, remain a strong independent marketplace — PPP is upside, not a precondition (canon risk #4).
- **Scale-up to Phase 4:** country-agnostic core extracted; local-config layer proven; unit economics positive.

## 7. Phase 4 — International replication

**Objective:** export the model to comparable island/small-state markets (canon Brand: "export to comparable island states").

### 7.1 Approach

- **Country-agnostic core + local configuration.** Separate the reusable engine (accounts, verification framework, listings, search/map, procedure-wizard shell, i18n) from country-specific config (laws, taxes, official sources, languages, partners).
- Each new country runs its **own bounded concierge pilot** (Phase-1 playbook) before scaling — the model does not assume transferability, it re-proves it locally.
- Local partners and local field teams; central product and platform.

### 7.2 Costs, team, KPIs

| Item | Detail (HYPOTHESIS) |
|------|---------------------|
| Team | Central platform team + per-country local concierge pods |
| Budget | Per-country pilot budgeted like Phase 1; central platform amortized |
| KPI focus | Time-to-pilot in a new country; share of core reused vs re-built; per-country contribution margin |

### 7.3 Gate / stop / scale-up

- **Gate:** first replication country clears its own Phase-1 conditions.
- **Stop:** if local config cost approaches rebuild cost, the core is not yet country-agnostic — return to Phase 3 engineering.
- **Scale-up:** repeatable per-country launch runbook exists.

## 8. Acquisition channels

All channels are **mobile-first** and **WhatsApp-centred**, reflecting the connectivity facts (73.5% internet, 115% mobile, 49.9% social — canon fact 1, **FACT/high**).

| Channel | How it works (Phase 1) | Owner | Cost profile | Notes |
|---------|------------------------|-------|--------------|-------|
| **WhatsApp distribution** | Broadcast lists + 1:1; listings, procedure tips and matches delivered via WhatsApp; support line is WhatsApp-first | Community lead | Low | Primary channel (canon: WhatsApp-oriented notifications) |
| **Community marketing** | Neighbourhood presence, trade associations, materials-supplier counters, local Facebook groups | Community lead | Low–med | Mindelo is walkable; density helps |
| **Events** | Small verified-professional meetups, foreign-buyer info sessions, a launch event | Pilot lead | Med (per §4.3) | 1–2/month; doubles as content + press |
| **Press** | Local media coverage of a neutral, trust-first platform; op-eds on de-risking property | Pilot lead | Low | *No fabricated endorsements* |
| **Ambassadors** | Respected agents/architects/lawyers who vouch and refer; verified badge as status | Community lead | Low (incentive) | Trust transfers through people |
| **Referral** | Structured referral for both professionals and prospects | Product | Low | Introduced once base exists (Phase 2) |

**RECOMMENDATION.** In Phase 1, weight effort toward WhatsApp + community + ambassadors (relationship channels) over paid ads. Paid acquisition is inefficient into a cold trust marketplace and is deferred to Phase 2+ once inventory is credible.

## 9. Onboarding strategy

Onboarding is **concierge-first** and personal (canon MVP). The progression from manual to product:

| Group | Phase 1 (manual) | Phase 2 (semi-auto) | Phase 3+ (self-serve) |
|-------|------------------|---------------------|-----------------------|
| Professionals | 1:1 session; team builds profile; L1–L3 by hand | Self-serve profile + concierge verification queue | Self-serve with automated L1–L2, human L3+ |
| Listings | Team photographs, writes, translates, publishes | Self-serve intake + concierge QA | Self-serve with QA sampling |
| Foreign prospects | WhatsApp concierge; human matchmaking | Guided self-serve + concierge for high value | Self-serve + concierge for land/high value |
| Information partners | Relationship-led, manual content | Templated publishing workflow | SLA publishing pipeline |

**Onboarding principles:** reduce friction to near-zero for the professional (we do the work), make the **verified badge** the reward, and capture willingness-to-pay signals during onboarding (offer Pro/Premium at the moment of demonstrated value).

## 10. Sales strategy

**RECOMMENDATION.** Ilhavista's early sales are **value-proven, not cold**. The team never sells a subscription before demonstrating a lead or a match.

- **Free tier first.** Every professional starts free with a verified profile (canon: Free tier).
- **Paywall on proven value.** Offer **Pro ≈ 2.500 CVE/mo (~€23)** or **Business ≈ 7.500 CVE/mo (~€68)** once a professional has received qualified leads or wants more visibility (canon business model).
- **Property monetization.** Free basic listing; **Premium ≈ 5.000 CVE (~€45)/30d** and Featured placement (always labelled Sponsored) for sellers wanting reach.
- **Verification fees** per level (L1–L5), tied to evidence and re-check burden.
- **Lead fees** optional; **facilitated-job take rate (5–10%)** deferred to Phase 3 (once escrow exists).
- **Guardrail:** paid tiers buy **visibility and tooling only** — never verification status, review scores, or official-info manipulation.

**Sales motion:** consultative and relationship-led in Phase 1 (the concierge team sells), transitioning to **product-led + inside sales** in Phase 2+ (self-serve upgrade prompts triggered by demonstrated value, e.g. lead volume).

## 13. Summary

Ilhavista's GTM is a **trust-seeding sequence**, not an advertising blitz. Phase 1 on São Vicente proves the loop with a small concierge team over WhatsApp; Phase 2 transfers and semi-automates it across islands; Phase 3 adds national coverage and institutional partnerships; Phase 4 exports a country-agnostic core. Every phase gates on evidence, and every monetization move follows demonstrated value.
